About Us

As a part of our Coder Foundry curriculum, we have been tasked to design a mortgage calculator to provide an estimate of a monthly mortgage payment. With this application you are able to input the loan amount, interest rate and loan term to calculate the monthly payment.

The monthly payment estimates are broken down by principal, interest, total interest and balance over the length of the loan. In addition, the calculator provides a chart symbolizing the decrease in the balance of the loan and interest payments over the length of the loan. Notice the scrolling table will identify each month’s financial outlook that includes Month, Payment, Principal, Interest, Total Interest and Balance. Lastly, a downloadable link has been provided for an excel version of the financial table.
